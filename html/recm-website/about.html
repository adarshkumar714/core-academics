About Us

Rajkiya Engineering College Mainpuri (RECM) shines as a symbol of academic excellence, dedicated to providing quality education and fostering a drive for distinction. Established by the Government of Uttar Pradesh in 2015, the college embarked on its journey with a single branch —Civil Engineering. It has since expanded its UG (B.Tech) program offerings to include branches like Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Computer Science and Engineering. Being a research Center, Institute is also Offering PhD in various courses.

Affiliated with Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow (formerly Uttar Pradesh Technical University, Lucknow), RECM is committed to growth and evolution. The college transitioned to its fully residential campus in Mainpuri District in 2017, offering a comprehensive infrastructure including Administrative Building, Various Academic Blocks, Workshop, Computer Center, Digital Library, Hostels, Students Activity Centre, College Canteen, Digital Studio, Incubation Center (StartUPs), Gym, Dispensary and more.

Adhering to the norms of AICTE, RECM boasts a modern, environmentally conscious campus, featuring well-designed structures with advanced laboratories, and a well-stocked Knowledge Library Center with physical books and digital resources. This environment nurtures a stimulating ambiance for learning and growth among students.

At the heart of RECM’s success are its succeeding Students, Alumni & distinguished faculty members, each faculty member hails from esteemed institutions, ensuring a strong academic foundation for the students. The college takes immense pride in its illustrious alumni who have excelled in various national exams, carving remarkable paths for themselves. Many have successfully achieved positions in prestigious roles like Officer in Indian Army, Indian Engineering Services (IES), Assistant Engineers (AE) etc. and have even secured admission to top-tier management institutions like the Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs) for MBA programs. Many serving in Top Notch Multinational firms. Additionally, several alumni have pursued further academic accomplishments, obtaining masters from renowned institutions such as IITs & NITs.

To parents, trust is valued at REC Mainpuri, and college is committed to entering student’s success. RECM welcomes aspiring engineers to Join our diverse and innovative community and embark on a remarkable college experience.
